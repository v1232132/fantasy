How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes between 5–10 business days depending on your location.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Please note that international orders may take longer to arrive.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept major credit cards and select cryptocurrencies.

Are your miniatures hand-painted?

All miniatures are shipped unpainted unless otherwise specified in the product description.

Can I return a product?

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unopened and in original condition.
